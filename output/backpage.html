Naked Cane Toad Causes Stir Near School, Blames 'Tropical Heatwave Frenzy'

Darwin DaveTroppo Correspondent

The normally tranquil environs of Billabong Elementary were thrown into disarray yesterday when a notoriously uninhibited Cane Toad was reportedly found 'completely naked' near the school grounds. Parents, including several ruffled Cockatoos, expressed outrage, claiming the Cane Toad was 'exposing himself to children'. When confronted by local authorities (a very patient Koala constable), the amphibian offender reportedly croaked, 'It's the tropical heatwave frenzy! The humidity, it just... makes my skin breathe!' He then attempted to explain his 'natural state' was simply a response to the searing sun. While the 'tropical heatwave frenzy' defense raised a few eyebrows, the Cane Toad was promptly escorted away, advised to seek cooler climes, and perhaps invest in some swim trunks.

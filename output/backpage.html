Narrabeen Nutjob Claims 'John Wick' Identity During Wild Pursuit

Darwin DaveTroppo Correspondent

In a truly baffling incident, a local Cane Toad, whose identity remains undisclosed, was apprehended after a high-speed pursuit through Narrabeen, claiming to be a distant relative of the famed anthropomorphic assassin, 'John Wick'. The chase, which involved several patrol vehicles and left a trail of confused Cockatoos in its wake, ended when the erratic amphibian veered into a local pond. Upon apprehension, the Cane Toad reportedly croaked, 'You don't understand, they took my little green pencil! The one I used to write poetry!' Darwin Dave reports from the front lines of weirdness: 'This fella was hopped up on something, or just plain bonkers. Even by our standards, that's a new level of trop-crazy. Good on the Bin Chicken traffic cops for corralling him before he caused any real mayhem!'

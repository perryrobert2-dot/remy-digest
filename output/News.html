Mysterious Second Fire Raises Alarm in Local Burrow Community

PuddlesSenior Reporter

The community is on high alert after a second fire in as many weeks has been deemed deliberate by authorities. The recent blaze, which gutted an abandoned shed near the communal foraging grounds, follows a similar incident last Tuesday. While no injuries have been reported, the incidents have sparked widespread concern among local Wombats and Echidnas. 'It's unsettling,' stated a local Echidna resident, 'to think someone is intentionally setting fires in our neighbourhood.' Bin Chicken investigators are sifting through the charred remains, seeking clues to the identity of the arsonist. Police advise residents to remain vigilant and report any suspicious activity.
